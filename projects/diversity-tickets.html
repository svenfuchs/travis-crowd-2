We support conferences and events by helping them to offer diversity tickets for underrepresented minorities in tech.

The idea

Many conferences wish to foster diversity in their audience by supporting attendees who might not have the means — financial or otherwise — to get to their conference without help. Having each conference set up their own diversity ticketing solution is time-consuming, so we’ve come up with a way to have conferences interested in offering diversity ticketing sign up on a platform which will handle all of the submissions for them.

The process

We’ve partnered with Berlin study group Rubymonstas to build together from the ground up an app which conference organisers worldwide will be able to use when working with us. Organisers can register their event online and set up a form to accept diversity ticketing submissions (as well as travel or accommodation grants if required).
